How can the Earth be saved
by our own hands

The current condition of the earth has been very challenging for the healthy quality of life because of the various pollution caused by our own hands. So, how can we save the mother earth we live on?

First of all, what we can do is reduce as much electricity usage as possible so that we can save more energy. Turning off the unused lights and switches is effective enough if everyone follows along. Another one is that, we can also try not to waste too much food so that they don't go as a waste which will turn into rubbish and cause pollutions.

Don't forget the 3 Rs, too!

  • Reduce the amount of energy consuming such as electricity and heat.
  • Reuse the things that are still in good conditions such as glass jars and plasic bottles.
  • Recycle the waste materials and process them into a perfect condition product.
